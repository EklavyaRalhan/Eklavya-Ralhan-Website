Places Must Visit

Australia

I visited Australia in 2018 when I was 6 years old.

Australia has amazing weather and tons of nature surrounding you. The food is good and the residential suburbs are cosy too.



Switzerland

I visited Switzerland in 2017 when I was 5 years old.

Switzerland was the first European country I visited and is the best country I have visited by far. The mountains are beautiful the weather is perfect, the food is amazing and the cities and villages were comfortable. Even the trains and trams were amazing!



UAE

I visited Switzerland in 2012 when I was a few months old.

I live an hour an a half away from Dubai in Abu Dhabi and everytime I go to Dubai I am always wowed by the tall skyscrapers there and the cool places you can visit. The communities are good to live in and you have unlimited choice of food from all around the globe. Even Abu Dhabi has amazing theme parks such as ferrari world,yas waterworld,warner bros and clymb.
